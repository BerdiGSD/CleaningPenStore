Shipping Policy

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days.

Tica Tide LLC is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier or our support team directly to file a claim. Please save all packaging material and damaged goods before filing a claim.

We currently do not ship outside the U.S.

Our Return & Refund Policy provides detailed information about options and procedures for returning your order.
